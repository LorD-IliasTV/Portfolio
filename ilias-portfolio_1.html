Who I Am

Two disciplines, one craftsman

A third-year Science des Données et Développement Informatique student who treats clean architecture and worldbuilding as the same skill, applied to different worlds.

The Engineer

I'm currently in my third year at ESTEM Casablanca, studying software engineering, Java/J2EE, Angular, Python for Data Science, Oracle database administration and web development.

During my internship at LAI Energie SARL in Mohammedia, I built a full-stack task management dashboard with real-time updates, role-based access and a live Kanban board — the kind of tool a team actually opens every morning.

The Worldbuilder

Outside the classroom, I'm building Yama: Half-Blood — a nine-chapter dark fantasy story with branching endings, a full cast, hand-drawn pixel sprites, and a browser-based 3D action RPG rendered in Three.js with real-time combo combat.

I've also prototyped a JoJo-inspired Roblox MMORPG with custom Stand mechanics and combo-chain systems in Lua — game design isn't a side hobby, it's the other half of how I think.
